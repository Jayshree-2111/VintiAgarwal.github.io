Follow me on
Vinti Agarwal

I am Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Information Systems at BITS Pilani and leading a GraphAI research group. My research focuses on developing techniques to transform a variety of data into graphs and learning their representation using machine learning. Applications include social recommender systems, natural language processing, computational biology and computational social health science.
